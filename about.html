About Safal Impex

Your trusted global sourcing agent and premium exporter from India.

Delivering Trust Across Borders

At Safal Impex, we specialize in bridging the gap between authentic Indian craftsmanship and the global market. Based in Pune, Maharashtra, we operate as both a premier exporter and a reliable global sourcing agent.

Our mission is to bring the finest quality products—ranging from intricate Barmer crafts and elegant Blue Pottery to high-grade Epoxy resin articles—to international buyers. We prioritize transparency, stringent quality assurance, and seamless logistics in every transaction.
